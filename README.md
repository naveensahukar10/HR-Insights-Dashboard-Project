📊 HR Analytics Dashboard
📌 Project Overview: The HR Analytics Dashboard is an Excel-based Business Intelligence solution designed to analyze employee
                     workforce data and identify key factors influencing employee attrition. The dashboard provides actionable 
                     insights into workforce demographics, department-wise turnover, job satisfaction, and employee retention trends.
                     By transforming raw HR data into meaningful visualizations, this dashboard enables HR professionals and management teams
                     to make data-driven decisions for improving employee engagement and reducing attrition.

🎯 Business Problem: Employee attrition can significantly impact organizational productivity, recruitment costs, and workforce stability. HR teams often struggle to:
- Track employee turnover effectively.
- Identify departments with high attrition.
- Understand demographic trends affecting retention.
- Monitor workforce composition and satisfaction levels.
- Generate insights for strategic HR planning.

💡 Business Solution
Developed an interactive Excel Dashboard that:
- Tracks key HR metrics in real time.
- Analyzes attrition across departments and job roles.
- Monitors workforce demographics.
- Identifies high-risk employee segments.
- Supports strategic workforce planning and retention initiatives.

Attrition Analysis
- R&D department experiences the highest employee attrition.
- HR department records the lowest attrition.
- Laboratory Technicians and Sales Executives show the highest turnover rates.
- Employees aged 25–34 contribute the largest share of attrition.
- Bachelor's Degree holders have the highest attrition count.

Recommendations
- Implement targeted retention programs in R&D and Sales departments.
- Improve career development opportunities for employees aged 25–34.
- Conduct regular employee engagement and satisfaction surveys.
- Strengthen onboarding and mentorship programs for new hires.
- Monitor job roles with consistently high turnover.
